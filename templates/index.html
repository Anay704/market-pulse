How to read this
Edge % shows how much mathematical advantage exists in a market. A positive edge means the combined YES + NO price is below $1.00 — buying both sides guarantees profit. A negative edge means the market maker is taking a spread. Higher volume markets tend to have lower spreads and more reliable pricing. For educational purposes only.
